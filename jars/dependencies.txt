The following libraries are needed to compile jgcgen:
 * Velocity engine + dependencies (http://velocity.apache.org/)
 * Commons-cli (http://commons.apache.org/cli/)
 * JEP (http://sourceforge.net/projects/jep/)

Place commons-cli-1.2.jar, velocity-1.7-dep.jar and jep-2.4.1.jar (or newer) in this directory.
